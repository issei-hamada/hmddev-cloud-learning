The team is building a comprehensive CLF-C02 exam preparation document. The draft structure is in `.spec/draft.md` with 17 chapters (ch0-ch16) plus 3 appendices (A-C). Target audience is IT beginners but reviewed from multiple personas.

**Why:** To provide a structured, Japanese-language study guide covering all 4 CLF-C02 domains.

**How to apply:** When reviewing individual chapter drafts, check against the overall structure in draft.md for consistency. Key recurring gaps found in initial draft review: missing Bedrock, CDK, Pricing Calculator, CloudShell. Watch for EBS multi-attach inaccuracy pattern and Aurora throughput claims needing MySQL/PostgreSQL distinction.
